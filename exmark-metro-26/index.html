Key Features

The Exmark Metro 26 is a top-of-the-line commercial walk-behind mower that is designed to provide excellent performance and durability. This mower is packed with advanced features that make it an excellent choice for professional landscapers and homeowners who are looking for a high-quality lawn mower that can handle tough mowing jobs. Here are some of the key features of the Exmark Metro 26:

Deck and Blade Assembly

The Exmark Metro 26 features a 26-inch cutting deck that is made of high-quality steel. The deck is designed to provide a precise and clean cut, and it is reinforced with a heavy-duty steel plate for added durability. The mower also features a high-lift blade assembly that is designed to provide maximum airflow and superior cutting performance.

Engine

The Exmark Metro 26 is powered by a Kawasaki FJ180V engine that provides excellent power and reliability. This engine is designed to provide maximum torque and efficiency, and it is equipped with a large-capacity air cleaner for improved performance.

Transmission and Drive System

The Exmark Metro 26 features a hydrostatic transmission that provides smooth and precise control over the mower’s speed and direction. The mower is also equipped with a self-propelled drive system that makes it easy to maneuver on all types of terrain.

Ergonomics and Comfort

The Exmark Metro 26 is designed to provide excellent ergonomics and comfort for the operator. The mower features an adjustable handlebar that can be adjusted to different heights for improved comfort, and it also has a cushioned operator platform that reduces fatigue and improves comfort during long mowing sessions.

Warranty

The Exmark Metro 26 is backed by a two-year limited warranty that covers the mower’s engine, transmission, and other key components. This warranty provides added peace of mind for buyers who are looking for a reliable and durable lawn mower.

Overall, the Exmark Metro 26 is an excellent choice for professional landscapers and homeowners who are looking for a high-quality and reliable lawn mower. With its advanced features and durable construction, this mower is designed to provide many years of reliable service and outstanding performance.

Performance

Performance is one of the most important factors to consider when buying a lawn mower, and the Exmark Metro 26 is no exception. Here are some of its key performance features:

Mowing Capacity and Cutting Quality

The Exmark Metro 26 has a 26-inch cutting deck that is designed to deliver a high-quality cut even in tough conditions. The deck is made of heavy-duty welded steel for durability and features a two-blade system that creates a powerful vacuum to lift and cut the grass. This allows the mower to handle thick grass and weeds with ease, leaving behind a clean, even cut.

Mulching and Bagging Performance

The Exmark Metro 26 is equipped with a side discharge chute for grass clippings, as well as a bagging system for those who prefer to collect their clippings. In addition, the mower can be converted to a mulching mower with the addition of an optional mulching kit. With the mulching kit installed, the mower can finely chop grass clippings and return them to the lawn, providing valuable nutrients and helping to keep the lawn healthy.

Maneuverability

One of the most impressive features of the Exmark Metro 26 is its maneuverability. The mower has a zero-turn radius, allowing it to turn on a dime and navigate tight spaces with ease. This is particularly useful for those with smaller lawns or for those who need to maneuver around obstacles like trees, flower beds, or other landscaping features.

Durability and Reliability

The Exmark Metro 26 is designed to be a durable and reliable lawn mower. It features a commercial-grade Kawasaki engine that is known for its performance and durability, as well as a heavy-duty transmission and drive system. The mower is also backed by a two-year limited warranty for added peace of mind.

Overall, the Exmark Metro 26 delivers impressive performance and is an excellent choice for those who want a high-quality, reliable lawn mower that can handle a variety of mowing conditions.

Maintenance and Care

Proper maintenance is essential for keeping any mower running in top condition. Here are some things to keep in mind for maintaining your Exmark Metro 26:

Maintenance Requirements and Schedule

The Exmark Metro 26 comes with a user manual that includes a maintenance schedule. It is recommended to follow the maintenance schedule to keep your mower running smoothly. Some of the routine maintenance tasks include checking the oil level, replacing the air filter, sharpening the blade, cleaning the deck, and checking the tire pressure.

It is important to perform these tasks on a regular basis to keep your mower in optimal condition.

Best Practices for Maintaining the Exmark Metro 26

Here are some best practices for maintaining your Exmark Metro 26:

Keep the Deck Clean

Regularly clean the deck after each use. This will prevent grass clippings and debris from building up and clogging the deck, which can affect cutting performance.

Sharpen the Blade

It is recommended to sharpen the blade every 25 hours of use. A dull blade can damage the turf and lead to an uneven cut. It is also important to balance the blade to prevent vibrations.

Check and Change the Oil

Check the oil level before each use and change the oil after every 100 hours of use or at least once a year. This will ensure the engine runs smoothly.

Replace the Air Filter

Replace the air filter every 100 hours of use or at least once a year. A clogged air filter can affect the engine’s performance.

Check the Tire Pressure

Check the tire pressure regularly and maintain the recommended pressure. Low tire pressure can cause uneven cutting and damage the turf.

By following these maintenance tips and best practices, you can ensure that your Exmark Metro 26 runs smoothly and lasts for many years.

Tips for Using and Maintaining the Exmark Metro 26

here are some additional tips for using and maintaining the Exmark Metro 26:

Keep the Blades Sharp

Dull blades can damage the grass and reduce the quality of the cut. It’s important to keep the blades sharp by sharpening them at least once a year or whenever they start to show signs of wear.

Use High-quality Fuel and Oil

The engine of the Exmark Metro 26 requires high-quality fuel and oil to run smoothly and last longer. Always use a high-octane fuel and a good quality oil that meets the manufacturer’s recommendations.

Clean the Mower After Each Use

Grass clippings, debris, and moisture can accumulate on the mower after each use, which can cause rust and corrosion. It’s important to clean the mower thoroughly after each use to remove any debris and prevent rust.

Store the Mower Properly

When the mowing season is over, it’s important to store the Exmark Metro 26 properly to protect it from the elements. Store the mower in a dry and cool place, and cover it with a tarp or a cover to prevent dust and moisture from getting in.

Check the Tire Pressure Regularly

The Exmark Metro 26 has pneumatic tires that require proper inflation to work properly. It’s important to check the tire pressure regularly and keep them properly inflated to ensure good traction and stability.

By following these tips, you can get the most out of your Exmark Metro 26 and keep it running smoothly for many years to come.

Can the Exmark Metro 26 Be Used for Commercial Applications?

Yes, the Exmark Metro 26 is designed for commercial use and is a popular choice among professional landscapers. It is built with durable components, such as a heavy-duty steel deck and a reliable engine, that can withstand frequent and heavy use.

The mower is also designed for efficient and precise cutting, making it well-suited for commercial applications where time and quality are of the essence.

Is the Exmark Metro 26 Easy to Transport and Store?

Yes, the Exmark Metro 26 is designed to be easily transported and stored. It features a compact and lightweight design that makes it easy to maneuver and transport. The mower also has folding handles that allow it to be stored in small spaces, such as a garage or shed.

The Exmark Metro 26 is designed to be fuel-efficient, thanks to its reliable and powerful engine. It uses a commercial-grade Kawasaki engine that provides excellent performance and fuel efficiency.
