Features

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is a powerful and efficient commercial-grade zero-turn riding lawn mower designed for professional landscapers and homeowners with large properties. It boasts a wide range of features and specifications that make it stand out among other models in the market.

Deck

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 has a durable 60-inch cutting deck made of 10-gauge steel, making it highly resistant to wear and tear. It features a foot-operated deck lift system that allows the user to easily adjust the cutting height, as well as a dial-adjusted cutting height selector that can be set to 15 different positions for maximum precision.

Engine

This lawn mower is powered by a reliable 24 horsepower Kawasaki FX730V engine that provides the necessary power to tackle tough mowing jobs with ease. It features a heavy-duty air filter and an efficient cooling system that prevents overheating during extended periods of use.

Controls

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 has user-friendly controls that are easily accessible and highly intuitive. It features a high-back adjustable seat with armrests, a dual-lever steering system that allows for maximum control and maneuverability, and an ergonomic control panel with keyless ignition, choke, and throttle controls.

Comfort and Accessibility

This lawn mower is designed with the operator’s comfort in mind. It features a suspension system that absorbs shocks and vibrations, reducing operator fatigue and ensuring a smoother ride.

The adjustable high-back seat and armrests provide maximum comfort during long mowing sessions. Additionally, the machine’s open platform design provides easy access to the engine and other vital components, making maintenance a breeze.

Warranty

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 comes with a 3-year limited warranty for residential use and a 2-year limited warranty for commercial use. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship, giving users peace of mind knowing they are investing in a high-quality product.

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is an impressive zero-turn riding lawn mower that offers top-notch features and specifications. From the durable cutting deck and powerful engine to the user-friendly controls and comfortable design, this machine is built to handle even the toughest mowing jobs with ease. Plus, the warranty ensures that users can confidently invest in a high-quality product that will last for years to come.

Pros of Owning a Gravely Pro Turn 160

Cut Quality

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 provides a superior cut quality due to its innovative cutting technology. The machine’s cutting deck is designed to create a vacuum-like effect, lifting the grass for an even cut. The cutting height can be adjusted on the fly to ensure a precise cut, even on uneven terrain.

Easy Maintenance and Access

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is designed for easy maintenance and access. The machine’s components are easily accessible, allowing for quick and simple maintenance. The deck can be removed without the use of tools, making it easy to clean and sharpen blades.

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is designed with a suspension system that provides a smooth and comfortable ride. Cons

Despite the many advantages of the Gravely Pro Turn 160, there are a few limitations that are worth mentioning. In this section, we will discuss the two main cons of this machine.

Oil Filter Placement

One of the biggest drawbacks of the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is the placement of the oil filter. It is located in a difficult-to-reach area of the engine, which makes it challenging to replace the filter.

This can be frustrating for users who need to change the oil frequently. However, it is worth noting that the oil filter can still be changed, but it will take a bit more time and effort.

Fuel Consumption

Another drawback of the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is its fuel consumption. Since it has a large engine, it consumes more fuel than smaller mowers. This can be a concern for users who are looking to save money on fuel costs.

However, it is worth noting that the Pro Turn 160 is designed for commercial use and is built to handle large properties. As a result, the higher fuel consumption is necessary for its power and performance.

In conclusion, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is an excellent commercial-grade mower with numerous advantages. However, the oil filter placement and fuel consumption are the two main drawbacks that users should consider before making a purchase. Despite these limitations, the Pro Turn 160 remains a top-performing mower that is built to last and provide excellent cutting quality.

Comparison

When it comes to commercial mowers in the same class as the Gravely Pro Turn 160, there are a few key competitors to consider, including the Exmark Lazer Z X-Series, Toro Z Master 2000 Series, and Hustler Super Z. Each of these mowers has its own unique advantages and disadvantages when compared to the Gravely Pro Turn 160.

The Exmark Lazer Z X-Series is known for its cutting-edge technology, including electronic fuel injection and RED Technology, which helps to reduce fuel consumption and emissions. However, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 boasts a more powerful engine, which allows for faster mowing speeds and improved performance on hills.

The Toro Z Master 2000 Series is a popular choice among landscapers due to its excellent maneuverability and comfortable seating. While the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is also easy to maneuver and comfortable to ride, it has a larger fuel tank and higher fuel efficiency, which means less time spent refueling and more time spent mowing.

Finally, the Hustler Super Z is known for its impressive speed and agility, but it lacks some of the features found on the Gravely Pro Turn 160, such as a high-back seat and a rollover protection system.

Overall, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 stands out among its competitors for its combination of power, speed, fuel efficiency, and comfort. While it may not have some of the high-tech features found on other mowers, it offers an excellent balance of performance and value.

Tips to Maintain This Mower

Use a Fuel Stabilizer

To prevent fuel-related issues and extend the life of your engine, it’s a good idea to add a fuel stabilizer to your gas tank. This can help prevent the gas from breaking down and causing problems with the engine.

Clean the Deck Regularly

To maintain optimal performance and prevent buildup of grass and debris, it’s important to clean the mower deck after each use. This can be done with a scraper or brush and can help prevent rust and damage to the deck.

Check and Replace Air Filters

The air filter is an important component of your mower’s engine and can impact its performance. Check the air filter regularly and replace it when it’s dirty or damaged. This can help prevent dirt and debris from getting into the engine and causing damage.

Check Tire Pressure

Proper tire pressure is important for a smooth ride and optimal performance. Check the tire pressure regularly and adjust it as needed to ensure even wear and a smooth ride.

Store the Mower Properly

When you’re not using your Gravely Pro Turn 160, it’s important to store it properly to prevent damage and ensure it’s ready to use when you need it. This may include storing it in a dry, protected area and covering it with a tarp or other protective cover. It’s also a good idea to remove the battery and store it separately in a cool, dry place.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Does the Gravely Pro Turn 160 Handle Slopes and Hills?

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is designed to handle slopes and hills with ease. Its low center of gravity and wide stance provide stability on inclines, while its powerful engine and durable transmission ensure it can maintain speed and cutting performance.

Additionally, the adjustable seat and ergonomic controls allow the operator to maintain comfort and control even on rough terrain.

Can the Gravely Pro Turn 160 Be Used for More Than Just Mowing Grass?

Yes, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 can be used for a variety of tasks beyond just mowing grass. Its sturdy frame and powerful engine make it a good choice for hauling heavy loads, while its easy maneuverability and cutting precision make it useful for landscaping and leaf removal.

The machine can also be equipped with additional attachments, such as snowblowers and snow plows, making it a versatile year-round tool.

How Long Does the Gravely Pro Turn 160’s Warranty Last?

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 comes with a comprehensive three-year limited warranty, which covers defects in materials and workmanship. The engine and hydro-gear transmission are also covered separately under manufacturer warranties.

However, it’s important to note that the warranty only applies if the machine is used and maintained according to the manufacturer’s guidelines.

Is the Gravely Pro Turn 160 Easy to Maintain?

Yes, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is designed with ease of maintenance in mind. Its open frame and wide footplate provide easy access to the engine, transmission, and other components for routine maintenance tasks like oil changes and filter replacements.

Additionally, the machine’s deck can be raised and locked in place for cleaning and blade maintenance.

How Does the Gravely Pro Turn 160 Compare to Other Zero-turn Mowers in Its Class in Terms of Noise Level?

The Gravely Pro Turn 160 is relatively quiet compared to other zero-turn mowers in its class, thanks in part to its high-quality sound-dampening materials and muffler. However, it’s important to note that all gasoline-powered mowers will produce some noise during operation, and operators should take proper precautions, such as using ear protection, when operating any machine.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is a reliable and powerful commercial zero-turn mower with several notable features and advantages. It offers exceptional cut quality, smooth ride, and easy maintenance access, which are crucial factors for landscapers and lawn care professionals.

While it has a few drawbacks like fuel consumption and oil filter placement, overall, the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is a dependable and durable machine. Customer feedback also suggests that it’s a top-performing mower, providing excellent value for money.

Compared to other commercial mowers in its class, it has unique features that set it apart. If you’re looking for a high-quality zero-turn mower that is built to last, then the Gravely Pro Turn 160 is an excellent choice to consider.
