Can My Landlord See My Electric Bill?

Edited byMinhazul Abedin May 22, 2023May 22, 2023

Your landlord can’t see your electric bill if you pay your supplier directly. However, if it’s a public utility assessed against the property, not the individual tenant, your landlord may request to see the bills.

You should check your tenancy agreement to find out if you have to pay your landlord for energy and on what terms. Ask your landlord for a copy of your tenancy agreement if you don’t have one.
