Why My Carrier Infinity Thermostat has No Power?

They are designed to be user-friendly and provide a range of features that can help you save energy and improve the comfort of your home.

Key functions of Carrier Infinity Thermostats

Here is a list of key functions of a Carrier Infinity Thermostats:

Setting Temperature Schedules

You can program your thermostat to automatically adjust the temperature at different times of the day or week. This can help you save energy by setting the temperature lower when you’re not home or asleep.

Monitoring And Controlling Humidity

Some Carrier Infinity thermostats have humidity sensors that allow you to monitor and control the humidity levels in your home. This can be especially helpful in areas with high humidity, which can lead to mold and other indoor air quality issues.

Controlling Ventilation And Airflow

Some Carrier Infinity thermostats also have features that allow you to control the ventilation and airflow in your home. This can help you improve indoor air quality and make your home more comfortable.

Monitoring And Maintaining Air Filters

Carrier Infinity thermostats often have an air filter indicator that alerts you when it’s time to change the air filter in your HVAC system. This can help you maintain the efficiency and effectiveness of your system.

Overall, Carrier Infinity thermostats are advanced, feature-rich devices that can help you manage and optimize the temperature, humidity, and air quality in your home.

Symptoms Of A Carrier Infinity Thermostat With No Power

If your Carrier Infinity thermostat is experiencing no power, you may notice one or more of the following symptoms:

Complete loss of display

If the thermostat is completely off, you may see no display or LED lights on the screen.

Inability To Control Or Monitor The HVAC System

Without power, you may not be able to use the thermostat to adjust the temperature or other settings of your HVAC system. You may also not be able to view the current temperature or other information on the thermostat’s display.

Lack of response to input

If the thermostat is powered on but not functioning properly, you may notice that it doesn’t respond to input from the buttons or control dial.

If you are experiencing any of these symptoms, it’s important to take steps to troubleshoot and fix the issue as soon as possible. Without a functioning thermostat, you may not be able to control or monitor your HVAC system, which can lead to discomfort and potentially even damage to your system.

Causes Of A Carrier Infinity Thermostat Losing Power

There are several possible causes of a Carrier Infinity thermostat losing power, including:

  1. Issue with wiring connections: If the wiring connections between the thermostat and the furnace are loose or damaged, the thermostat may not receive power.
  2. Fuse Blown On Furnace Control Board: The furnace control board may have a fuse that protects the system from electrical surges. If this fuse is blown, the thermostat may lose power.
  3. Tripped circuit breaker or blown fuse in power supply: If the circuit breaker or fuse that powers the furnace trips or blows, the thermostat may lose power.
  4. Faulty thermostat: In some cases, the thermostat itself may be faulty and need to be replaced.

It’s important to identify and fix the root cause of the power loss in order to restore proper function to your Carrier Infinity thermostat.

Troubleshooting Steps For A Carrier Infinity Thermostat With No Power

If your Carrier Infinity thermostat is experiencing no power, you can try the following troubleshooting steps:

  • Check wiring connections at thermostat and furnace: Make sure that all of the wiring connections between the thermostat and the furnace are securely fastened and not damaged.
  • Check and replace fuse on furnace control board: If the fuse on the furnace control board is blown, you will need to replace it with a new one of the same rating.
  • Check Power Supply And Reset Circuit Breaker Or Replace Blown Fuse: Make sure that the circuit breaker or fuse that powers the furnace is not tripped or blown. If it is, reset the circuit breaker or replace the fuse.
  • Consult User Manual For Specific Troubleshooting Instructions: If you’re unable to fix the power issue with these steps, it may be helpful to consult the user manual for your Carrier Infinity system for more specific troubleshooting instructions. The manual may provide additional guidance on identifying and fixing the issue.

If you’re still unable to resolve the power issue after trying these steps, it may be best to consult a professional HVAC technician for further assistance.

How Do I Reset My Carrier Infinity Thermostat?

Step 1: Flip Down the Door at the Base of the Infinity™ Control

This step involves opening the thermostat’s control panel by flipping down the door located at the base of the Infinity™ Control. This allows access to the thermostat’s settings and controls.

Step 2: Press the Basic Button

Pressing the BASIC button brings you to the basic settings of the thermostat. This is where you can adjust the time, date, and temperature settings for your thermostat.

Step 3: Adjust the Highlighted Hour Setting Using the Time (+-) Button

The HOUR setting is the current hour on the thermostat. By using the (+-) button, you can adjust the hour to the correct time.

Step 4: Press Scroll to Highlight Minute

Pressing the SCROLL button will move you to the next setting, in this case, the MINUTE setting.

Step 5: Adjust the Minute Setting Using the Time (+-) Button

The MINUTE setting is the current minute on the thermostat. By using the (+-) button, you can adjust the minute to the correct time.

Step 6: Press Scroll to Highlight Day

Explanation: Pressing the SCROLL button will move you to the next setting, in this case, the DAY setting.

Step 7: Adjust the Current Day Setting Using the Time (+-) Button

The DAY setting is the current day on the thermostat. By using the (+-) button, you can adjust the day to the correct date.

Step 8: Press Scroll to Highlight Humidity

Pressing the SCROLL button will move you to the next setting, in this case, the HUMIDITY setting. This setting allows you to control the humidity levels in your home.

Step 9: Press the Red Heat Button to Set the Heating Humidity

Pressing the red HEAT button will allow you to set the desired humidity level for when the thermostat is in heating mode.

Step 10: Adjust the Desired Heating Humidity Using Either (+-) Button

Using the (+-) buttons, you can adjust the heating humidity level to your desired setting.

Step 11: Press the Blue Cool Button to Set the Cooling Humidity

Pressing the blue COOL button will allow you to set the desired humidity level for when the thermostat is in cooling mode.

Step 12: Adjust the Desired Cooling Humidity Using Either (+-) Button

Using the (+-) buttons, you can adjust the cooling humidity level to your desired setting.

Step 13: Press Basic or Close the Door to Exit

Once you have finished adjusting the settings to your liking, you can either press the BASIC button to exit back to the main menu or simply close the door to save your changes and exit the settings menu.

Things You Need to Check on the Carrier Infinity Thermostat

Here are some additional tips that may be helpful if you are troubleshooting a Carrier Infinity thermostat with no power:

Check The Batteries

If your Carrier Infinity thermostat uses batteries, make sure that they are not dead or low. Replace the batteries if necessary.

Check The Thermostat’s Settings

If the thermostat is turned off or in “sleep” mode, it may appear to have no power. Check the thermostat’s settings to ensure that it is turned on and in the correct mode.

Check The Thermostat’s Wiring

If you’re experiencing issues with the thermostat’s display or buttons, it’s possible that there is an issue with the wiring. Check the wiring connections at the thermostat and at the furnace to ensure that all wires are securely connected and not damaged.

Clean The Thermostat

If the thermostat’s display or buttons are not functioning properly, it’s possible that they may be dirty or covered in dust. Use a soft cloth to gently clean the thermostat’s display and buttons.

Check The HVAC System

If you’re having issues with the thermostat’s ability to control the HVAC system, it’s possible that there may be an issue with the system itself. Check the system’s wiring and components to ensure that everything is functioning properly.

Consult A Professional

If you’re unable to resolve the issue with your Carrier Infinity thermostat, it may be helpful to consult a professional HVAC technician for further assistance. They can diagnose and fix any issues with your thermostat or HVAC system.

Conclusion

It’s important to troubleshoot and fix any issues with a Carrier Infinity thermostat that is experiencing no power. Without a functioning thermostat, you may not be able to control or monitor your HVAC system, which can lead to discomfort and potentially even damage to your system.

To troubleshoot a Carrier Infinity thermostat with no power, you can try the following steps:

  1. Check wiring connections at the thermostat and furnace to ensure that all wires are securely connected and not damaged.
  2. Check and replace the fuse on the furnace control board if necessary.
  3. Check the power supply and reset the circuit breaker or replace a blown fuse if necessary.
  4. Consult the user manual for your Carrier Infinity system for more specific troubleshooting instructions.

By following these steps, you can help ensure that your Carrier Infinity thermostat is functioning properly and effectively controlling your HVAC system.
