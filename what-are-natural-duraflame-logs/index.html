Keep Your Fire Logs Cut to Size

Duraflame logs are cut from a single piece of wood that has been dried for several months. The size and shape of the log are determined by how much heat it will produce over time.

Clean The Chimney After Each Use

Cleaning the chimney after each use is crucial to prevent dangerous fire conditions and ensure a safe indoor environment. A Chimney Sweep can help clean your chimney quickly and easily, making sure your home is in tip-top shape all winter long.
