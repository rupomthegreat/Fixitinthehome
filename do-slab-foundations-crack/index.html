Do Slab Foundations Crack?

Shrinkage and crack formation can occur in any type of slab foundation, but it is more common with concrete or asphalt slabs. There are many causes for shrinkage and crack formation in a slab foundation, including weathering, improper installation techniques, and water damage.

The most common way to detect shrinkage or crack formation in a slab foundation is by using a percussive testing method.
