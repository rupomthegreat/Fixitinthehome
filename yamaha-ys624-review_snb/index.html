While the Yamaha YS624 snow blower is a reliable and durable machine, some common issues can occur with regular use. Overall, the Yamaha YS624 snow blower is a reliable and durable machine built with quality materials that guarantee its longevity. With proper maintenance and care, this machine can provide exceptional snow clearing performance for years.
