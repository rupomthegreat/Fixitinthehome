Does it matter if live and neutral are reversed?

The polarity of a battery determines which terminal is positive and which is negative. The terms “live” and “neutral” refer to the direction of current flow in the battery.

When you connect an electrical circuit, electrons flow from the positive terminal (the live wire) to the negative terminal (the neutral wire).
