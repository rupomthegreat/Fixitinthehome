Low levels of refrigerant can cause the compressor to freeze and lock up. Dirty coils can cause the unit to lock up due to an inability to dissipate heat. Clogged or dirty filters can restrict air flow, causing the compressor to overheat and lock up.

Kinked or restricted refrigerant lines can cause the compressor to lock up due to a drop in pressure. Excessive vibration can cause components to become loose and stop working. Improper wiring can lead to the compressor failing due to an overload of electricity.
